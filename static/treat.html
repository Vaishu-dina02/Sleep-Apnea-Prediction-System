Primary/First-Line Treatments

  • CPAP (Continuous Positive Airway Pressure)

    • CPAP delivers air pressure through a mask while you sleep, keeping your airway open...

  • Oral Appliances

    • These devices help keep the airway open by adjusting the jaw or tongue position...


    Secondary/Alternative Treatments

  • Positional Therapy

    • For those with positional sleep apnea, avoiding sleeping on the back may help.

  • Surgery

    • Considered when other treatments fail...
