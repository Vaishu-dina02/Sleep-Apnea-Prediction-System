About Us

Understanding Sleep Apnea

Sleep apnea is a serious yet often undiagnosed condition that disrupts breathing during sleep, leading to fatigue, health complications, and decreased quality of life. Our goal is to provide an AI-driven solution that helps individuals assess their risk and take proactive steps toward better sleep health.

Our AI-Powered Prediction Model

We leverage cutting-edge Artificial Intelligence to analyze user-inputted data and determine the probability of having sleep apnea. This model evaluates multiple factors and provides a detailed percentage-based analysis to help users make informed health decisions.

Personalized Nutrition Guidance

Beyond sleep apnea detection, our platform integrates a smart nutrition prediction system. Based on your health profile, our AI suggests personalized dietary recommendations that can help manage and improve sleep quality.
