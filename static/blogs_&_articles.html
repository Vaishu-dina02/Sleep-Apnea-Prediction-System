Sleep Apnea In Women


Women with sleep apnea are more prone to breathing disruptions during
REM sleep (about 20% of total sleep time).While this might mean fewer
overall apneas compared to men, the apneas women experience during
REM sleep tend to be more severe, lasting longer and causing greater drops
in blood oxygen levels.

SOURCE : ITAMAR
